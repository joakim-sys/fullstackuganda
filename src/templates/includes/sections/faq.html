  • How long does it take to build a website?

    The timeline for building a website varies depending on the complexity of the project. Simple websites can typically be completed within a few weeks, while more complex projects may take several months. We'll work closely with you to establish a timeline that meets your needs.

  • What is the cost of building a website?

    The cost of building a website depends on various factors, including the size of the site, features required, and level of customization. We offer flexible pricing options tailored to your budget and project requirements. Contact us for a personalized quote.

  • Can you help with website maintenance after the launch?

    Yes, we offer website maintenance services to ensure your site remains up-to-date, secure, and performing optimally. Our maintenance plans include regular updates, backups, security monitoring, and technical support to keep your website running smoothly.

  • Do you provide website hosting services?

    While we do not directly provide hosting services, we can recommend reliable hosting providers based on your needs and budget. We'll assist you in setting up your hosting account and ensure seamless integration with your website.

  • Will my website be mobile-friendly?

    Yes, all websites we build are designed to be fully responsive, ensuring optimal viewing and functionality across devices of all sizes, including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

  • Can you integrate third-party services into my website?

    Absolutely! Whether you need to integrate payment gateways, social media platforms, analytics tools, or any other third-party services, we have the expertise to seamlessly integrate them into your website to enhance its functionality and meet your business goals.

  • How do I update content on my website?

    We provide easy-to-use content management systems (CMS) such as Django and Wagtail CMS, allowing you to update content, images, and other elements of your website without any technical knowledge. We'll provide training and ongoing support to ensure you're comfortable managing your website's content.
